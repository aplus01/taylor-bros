Who We Are

Taylor Brothers Construction is fully licensed with a Limited Builders License through the employment of David B. Taylor, allowing us to take on Commercial or Residential projects up to $750,000. Our efforts have been focused on home upgrades, remodeling, and commercial upfits.

The Taylor family has been in construction for over thirty years, starting with PHC Construction. The retirement of Bill Taylor spurred the birth of Taylor Brothers Construction, LLC. With the knowledge gained from a lifetime of construction, Brian and David began a company with the needs and ideas of the client first.
